Project information

  • Tools: Python,HTML,CSS,AWS Lambda+API Gateway

At the beginning of the covid19, it was speculated that the demand for masks at pharmacies would increase, and the website would also need to increase traffic.
I proactively proposed to the company to develop a mask inventory search website, hoping to increase website traffic and exposure through this.
Through the development of real-time capture of mask inventory information, and a clear card-type interface with keywords,
This website had received 2.5 million traffic in three months, ranking among the top three in google searches, and has been shared by technology news website Techbang (Link), also increasing brand awareness.
